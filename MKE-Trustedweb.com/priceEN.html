Budget

The Budget package offers a cost-effective solution for those in need of a simple yet functional website. For only €250 (excl. VAT), you get a website with a custom design and one page. This package is ideal for freelancers, creatives, and small businesses looking to establish online visibility quickly at an affordable price.

Standard

Our Standard package is perfect for those needing more functionality and space. For €500 (excl. VAT), you'll receive a website with a custom design and five pages, including features such as a blog application to share regular content. This package is suitable for small businesses and startups looking to establish a professional online presence.

Custom

For those seeking a tailor-made solution that perfectly aligns with their unique needs, we offer the Custom package. With prices starting from €750 (excl. VAT), you'll get a website with a custom design and the flexibility to start from a single page. This package is ideal for businesses looking to provide a personalized online experience, including e-commerce functionality for a webshop.

Subscription

Our Subscription is the ultimate choice for those seeking flexibility and convenience, but keep in mind that you'll need to commit to a minimum 24-month contract. This package is perfect for freelancers, bloggers, and small businesses looking to start and grow without high initial costs.
